    # Carousel & 轮播

    # 使用方法

    • 设置autoply属性可实现自动播放
    • 鼠标悬停会停止自动播放，移开继续播放
    • 点击圆点手动跳转
    <o-carousel :selected.sync="select" autoplay>  
  <carousel-item name="1">
    <div class="box">1</div>
  </carousel-item>
  <carousel-item name="2">
    <div class="box">2</div>
  </carousel-item>
  <carousel-item name="3">
    <div class="box">3</div>
  </carousel-item>
  <carousel-item name="4">
    <div class="box">4</div>
  </carousel-item>
</o-carousel>

  retrun {select: ''}
